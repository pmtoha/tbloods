Who We Are

Tblood is a nonprofit blood donation network founded in 2024 by a group of visionary students.

Our Mission

We’re here to ensure that every patient who needs blood finds it fast. Through outreach, education, and innovative tech, we’re reshaping how communities think about donation.

What We Do

  • Coordinate monthly blood drives in urban and rural areas
  • Run digital campaigns to educate youth about the power of donation
  • Offer a 24/7 donor-matching and emergency response system
  • Partner with hospitals and clinics to bridge urgent supply gaps
  • Maintain a verified donor registry with location and blood type filters

Get Involved

Whether you want to donate, volunteer, or support the cause, we welcome you. Visit our Facebook page or email us directly — every action counts.
