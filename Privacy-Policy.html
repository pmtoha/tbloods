Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 15, 2025

Student Initiative Notice

tbloods.online is a student-run blood donation initiative. We are not a registered NGO or commercial organization. This privacy policy reflects our commitment to your privacy as a non-commercial, educational project.

We Collect No Personal Data

Our website is designed to provide information about blood donation and coordinate voluntary efforts. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from our visitors.

Information We Do Not Collect

To be completely clear about our practices:

  • We do not collect payment or financial information
  • We do not collect location data or device information
  • We do not use any analytics or tracking tools

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies on our website. This means:

  • No authentication cookies
  • No analytics or performance cookies
  • No advertising or targeting cookies
  • No social media cookies

You can browse our website completely anonymously without any tracking of your activities.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites, such as:

  • Official blood bank websites
  • Health organizations
  • Educational resources about blood donation
  • Social Media

Please note that we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies when visiting them.

Data Security

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no risk of your information being compromised through our website. We maintain appropriate security measures for our website infrastructure to ensure its availability.

Blood Donation Coordination

If you choose to participate in our blood donation initiatives through offline means:

  • Any information sharing would occur through direct, voluntary communication
  • We follow ethical guidelines for handling donor information
  • We respect donor privacy and confidentiality
  • We do not maintain databases of donor information on this website

Children's Privacy

Our website is accessible to everyone, but we do not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy as our student initiative evolves. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
